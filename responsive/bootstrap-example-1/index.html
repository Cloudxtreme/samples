ABOUT MBA.PM

The Schedule

The MBA.PM typically takes 33 months to complete, but may be extended up to five years. We have built this additional flexibility into the program to accomodate the demands of participants' busy professional lives. Classes are held in the evenings, twice a week.

The Faculty

Through the MBA.PM program, participants have access to leading scholars and practioners from a wide array of business backgrounds and disciplines. The experience and current work of Marshall's distinguished faculty extend well beyond the lecture hall: Our faculty serve as consultants to a broad range of organizations, from Fortune 500 companies to start-ups. Their accomplishments drive Marshall's innovation and keep students connected to the latest business practices while fostering a dynamic learning environment.

The Locations

During the first year, participants study at USC's University park campus in Los Angeles or at our Orange County Center in Irvine. At either location, students benefit from access to USC Marshall's internationally renowed faculty and superb facilities. Second and third year classes are held in Los Angeles.

The Network

The MBA.PM program connects students to an invaluable resource: their peers. Participants join colleagues from a broad range of industries and backgrounds, working toogether to hone their skills and expand their leadership potential. Graduates take advantage of the power of the Trojan network, with 198,000 alumni worldwide, which includes USC Marshall's 70,000-plus alumni in 90 countries. USC alumni hold positions of influence around the globe.

GLOBAL EXPERIENCE

In a competitive international marketplace, business professionals need every advantage. PM.GLOBE — USC Marshall's unique international study and travel experience creates unique opportnities to engage in the dynamics of global busniess. A required component of the MBA.PM program, PM.GLOBE exposes students to the challenges of doing business in the Pacific Rim. Combining analytical training with firsthand observation and experience, the program enables participants to spend an intensive week in countries such as China, Japan, Thailand and Jorea, deepening their knowledge of economic, institutional and cultrual issues, and preparing for success in a world with shrinking border.

Advanced skills. Enhanced expertise. Tremendous flexibilitty.

USC Marshall's MBA program for proffesionals and Managers (MBA.PM) affords partici[anrs all the benefits of an exceptional graduate business program while accomodating business schedules. Students can remain fully employed as they pursue their degree.

Much like Marshall's full-time MBA program, the MBA.PM program integrates a robust core curriculum with a broad range of specialized coursework that allows participants to pursue their interests and respond to the changinf needs of domestic and global business. First-year students may attend class either in Los Angeles or Orange County. Second and third year classes are offered on our Los Angeles campus. MBA.PM students have access to USC's renowned faculty, work with colleagues who have diverse backgrounds and goals, and participate in an intensive one-week study trip abroad.
